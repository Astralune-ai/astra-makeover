# Australian materials & suppliers (reference)

The board prompt already prefers commercially-available AU materials at a medium budget and
labels prices as "Estimated Sydney Market Price". This list is for context / sanity-checking
the board's callouts — it is not injected separately.

**Cabinetry surfaces:** Polytec · Laminex
**Tiles / splashback:** Beaumont Tiles · National Tiles
**Paint:** Dulux · Taubmans · Haymes Paint

**Lighting:** Beacon Lighting
**Furniture / styling:** Freedom · King Living · Early Settler · Temple & Webster · IKEA Australia
**Hardware / general:** Bunnings

**Pricing rules (already in the prompt):**
- All-in costs = supply + install unless noted; quote GST-inclusive (AU residential norm).
- Never fabricate an exact official supplier price → label "Estimated Sydney Market Price".
- Include a contingency (10%, or 15% if complex). State assumptions (plumbing/electrical/
  structure retained, no asbestos, lift access, standard labour rates).
- Estimates are indicative, subject to a site measure — never guarantee a future sale price.
